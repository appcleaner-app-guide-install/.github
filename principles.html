Principles for Greener Software

Measure

What gets measured gets improved. Track energy, time, and resource usage.

Avoid Unused Work

Cache aggressively, debounce tasks, and avoid polling.

Do Less, Later

Batch operations and align heavy work with user intent and device state.

Ship Lean

Minimize bundle size and image weight; prefer vector art and modern formats.

Right-Size Infra

Autoscale, pick efficient runtimes, and reduce idle compute.

Design for Longevity

Build maintainable systems that avoid frequent rewrites.
